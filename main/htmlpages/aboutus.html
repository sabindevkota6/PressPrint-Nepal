ABOUT US

Who Are We?

Pressprint Nepal

Pressprint Nepal is an authorized distributor of printers in Nepal. We are a group of young entrepreneurs working together as a team to provide quality and reliability to our customers. We sell varieties of printers from various well-known multi-national brands. We can ensure that our products are one hundred percent original. Our aim is to provide the best quality products to our customers at the most affordable price.

Our History

Pressprint Nepal is in the market since 2020. Four of us founding members established the store back in 2020 and are still working actively in the store in our particular positions. We started off with a small space and less than 10 printers in stock. And with continious hardwork and dedication of us and our teammates we have now become one of the biggest authorized distributor of printers in Nepal in just 4 years with 7 outlets in different cities of Nepal.
